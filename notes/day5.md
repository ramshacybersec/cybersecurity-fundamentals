# Day 5 - Nmap Basics

## What is Nmap?
Nmap is a network scanning tool used to discover hosts, open ports, and running services.

## Commands Learned
- nmap <target-ip>
- nmap -sV <target-ip>
- nmap -A <target-ip>

## What I Learned
Today I learned how to scan systems, identify open ports, and detect services using Nmap.
